Checkly Privacy Policy

Effective July 19, 2026

Checkly does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data. Period.

Calendar data

Checkly reads and writes events in your device's calendar solely to provide its features. Calendar data never leaves your device and is never shared with the developer or any third party.

Notifications

Checkly schedules local notifications on your device for event reminders and overdue to-dos. These are generated entirely on-device.

Analytics and tracking

Checkly contains no analytics, no advertising, no tracking, and no third-party SDKs. It makes no network connections.
